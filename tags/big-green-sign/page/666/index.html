I-15 North - Welcome To Arizona

Welcome To Arizona on I-15 north at the Nevada-Arizona state line. The larger sign says Arizona: The Grand Canyon State Welcomes You. The smaller sign says Arizona State Line. The smaller sign appears on most Arizona interstates entering or leaving Arizona.

I-15 North - Distance Marker

Last distance marker in Nevada on I-15 north. Distance to Littlefield (Arizona), St. George (Utah), and Salt Lake City (Utah). The Arizona Welcome Sign can be seen in the background.

BL-15 North (Sandhill Blvd) at I-15

Northern end of Mesquite business loop at I-15. Use I-15 north for St. George (Utah); use I-15 south for Las Vegas.
