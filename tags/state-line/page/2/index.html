Signs From

I-10 West - Welcome To New Mexico

Welcome to New Mexico - Land of Enchantment on I-10 west. Taken at the Texas - New Mexico State Line.

Anthony Dona Ana County New Mexico United States

OR-140 West - Welcome To Oregon

Welcome to Oregon on OR-140 west. Taken at the Oregon-Nevada State Line.

Harney County Oregon United States

US-197 South - Welcome To Oregon

Welcome to Oregon on US-197 south in The Dalles. The Oregon welcome sign is about half a mile from the actual state line on the bridge over the Columbia River.

The Dalles Wasco County Oregon United States

I-15 North - Welcome To Arizona

Welcome To Arizona on I-15 north at the Nevada-Arizona state line. The larger sign says Arizona: The Grand Canyon State Welcomes You. The smaller sign says Arizona State Line. The smaller sign appears on most Arizona interstates entering or leaving Arizona.

Mohave County Arizona United States

I-15 South - Welcome To Arizona

Welcome to Arizona on I-15 south at the Arizona-Utah Border. The sign reads Arizona: The Grand Canyon State Welcomes You.

Mohave County Arizona United States

Moffat County 10N - Wyoming-Colorado State Line

Moffat County 10N at the Wyoming-Colorado State Line. The mile marker below the highway shield is a standard Wyoming mile marker, and is for WY-430.

Moffat County Colorado United States

Entering Moffat County

Entering Moffat County Colorado at the Utah-Colorado State Line.

Moffat County Colorado United States

I-80 East - Welcome To Utah

Welcome to Utah on I-80 east. Each region in Utah has its own type of welcome sign. The welcome sign for the northwestern part of Utah shows a picture of Salt Lake City.

Wendover Tooele County Utah United States

US-101 North - Welcome To Oregon

Welcome to Oregon on US-101 north at the California line.

Curry County Oregon United States

US-20/US-26 East - Oregon Thanks You

Oregon Thanks You. Come Back Soon. Entering Canyon County (Idaho) on US-20/US-26 east at the Oregon/Idaho line.

Nyssa Malheur County Oregon United States
