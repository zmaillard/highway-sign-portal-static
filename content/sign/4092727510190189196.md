US-395 north at Exit 39 - US-50/Dayton/Fallon.  Continue north on I-580/US-395 for Reno.  The US-50/US-395 split ends here.  I-580 begins here northbound, although it is not listed on the overhead.  The first I-580 shield is north of this interchange.
