US-12 East at US-95.  Merge left for US-95 north to Moscow and Coeur d'Alene.  Use right lanes for US-12 East/US-95 South/Missoula (Montana)/Boise.  US-95 south joins US-12 east for about 10 miles eastbound along the Clearwater River.
