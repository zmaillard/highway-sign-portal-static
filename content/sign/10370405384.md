BL-25 North (Grand Ave.) at 7th Ave.  NM-518 used to run down 7th St. in Las Vegas.  Now NM-518 ends at Mills Ave., north of here, and the preferred route through Las Vegas is to take Grand Ave. north to Mills Ave.
