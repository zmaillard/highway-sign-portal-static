Distance marker on ID-87 north.  Distance to Quake Lake, Ennis, and Helena.  All three of these destinations are in Montana.  ID-87 is a short state highway that follows the east shore of Henrys Lake, before climbing to the Continental Divide at Raynolds Pass on the Idaho-Montana line.
