BL-90 East at US-14A.  Turn right for US-14 East to Spearfish Canyon and Cheyenne Crossing.  Continue ahead for BL-90 east for Deadwood.  Deadwood is not directly assessable via BL-90 or I-90, but is the preferred route from Spearfish.
