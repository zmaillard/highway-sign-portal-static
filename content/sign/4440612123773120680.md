I-70 west approaching Exit 288 - BL-70/US-40/Colfax Ave.  Exit 288 is a left exit; merge right to continue west on I-70 to Denver.  US-40 leaves I-70 westbound here, and follows Colfax Ave. through Denver.  US-40 rejoins I-70 near Golden.  This is the eastern end of the Denver business loop.
