Historic Oregon US-20/US-26 Shields

Historic Oregon US-20/US-26 shields in downtown Nyssa. This photo was taken in 2001, and were replaced with newer shields sometime in the early 2000’s.

Nyssa Malheur County Oregon United States

I-84 at Exit 374 - OR-201/Bus. US-30 Jct.

I-84 East (Exit 374) offramp at OR-201/Bus. US-30 Jct. Use OR-201 north to State Park, Payette (Idaho), and Weiser (Idaho). Use OR-201 South/Bus. US-30 for Ontario.

Ontario Malheur County Oregon United States

I-84 West - Exit 376

I-84 west approaching Exit 376A and B. Use Exit 376A for US-20/US-26 to Lake Owyhee. Use Exit 376B for State Police.

Ontario Malheur County Oregon United States

OR-201 North at OR-52

OR-201 north at OR-52 east to Payette (Idaho). Continue north on OR-201 to Weiser (Idaho) and Baker City.

Malheur County Oregon United States

OR-201 South - Owyhee River

OR-201 South - Owyhee River in Malheur County.

Malheur County Oregon United States

Succor Creek Rd at OR-201

Succor Creek Road north at OR-201. Use OR-201 south to Homedale (Idaho) and Caldwell (Idaho). Use OR-201 north to Adrian and Nyssa. Rural intersections in Oregon often use these types of direction signs.

Malheur County Oregon United States

Begin US-30 at US-101 (Former Begin of US-26)

Begin US-30 at US-101 (Former Begin of US-26). Turn left for US-30 west to Astoria City Center; turn right to continue south on US-101 and for US-26 (Former) to Seaside. The new starting point of US-26 is in Seaside.

Astoria Clatsop County Oregon United States

End OR-126 East at US-26

End OR-126 East at US-26 in Prineville. Use US-26 east to City Center and John Day; US-26 west to Madras and Portland.

Prineville Crook County Oregon United States

OR-126 East - Distance Marker

Distance marker on OR-126 east. Distance to Redmond and Prineville.

Sisters Deschutes County Oregon United States

OR-126 East at O'Neil Highway

OR-126 East at O’Neil Highway jct. to O’Neil and Terrebonne (OR-370). Continue on OR-126 east to Prineville and Mitchell.
